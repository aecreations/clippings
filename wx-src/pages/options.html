Sync Clippings

You can synchronize your clippings with Thunderbird or other Firefox installations by putting the clippings you want to share into a special "Synced Clippings" folder. These clippings will appear in the Synced Clippings folder in those applications. You can choose which clippings you want to sync by moving or copying them into the Synced Clippings folder in Clippings Manager.

The Synced Clippings folder will be automatically created once Sync Clippings is set up.

Setting Up

Sync Clippings needs to be turned on in the applications you want to synchronize your clippings with. Sync Clippings is supported on Firefox 60 and newer, and Thunderbird 60 and newer (with Clippings for Thunderbird 5.6 installed).

In addition, the Sync Clippings helper app needs to be installed on your system to keep your Synced Clippings folder contents up to date. It is available for download from the AE Creations website. Once installed, it will be launched automatically when Firefox is started, and will run in the background.

In Firefox, activate Sync Clippings from the Clippings preferences page by clicking Turn On Sync. You will be prompted to choose a file location on your system where the synced clippings data will be saved, if this was not set already.
