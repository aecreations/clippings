Clippings 6 – Now Available

Clippings 6 is a WebExtension that is compatible with Firefox 57 and newer. If you are planning on upgrading Firefox to a newer version, you should also prepare for upgrading Clippings to version 6.

Get Ready

Once Clippings is upgraded to the new WebExtension, there is an extra step you will need to take to bring your clippings over. To prepare for the upgrade, you should save a backup of your clippings.

Hint: You can backup your clippings as many times as you want, until Clippings is upgraded to version 6.

You may want to do this to ensure you have the most up-to-date backup.

Backup Your Clippings

  1. Open Clippings Manager.
  2. Click Tools → Backup.
  3. Select a location on your system to export to (such as the Desktop or your Documents folder).

Make sure you remember where you saved the backup. You will need to locate the backup clippings file once Clippings is upgraded.

➤ Click here to open Clippings Manager and save a backup now.

After Upgrading to Clippings 6

After the upgrade is complete, follow the on-screen instructions to bring your clippings over to Clippings 6, and you'll be up and running again.
