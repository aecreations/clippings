Coming Soon

Clippings 6

The next version of Clippings will be a WebExtension which will be compatible with Firefox 57. Clippings 6 is planned for release in November 2017, at around the same time as Firefox 57 (also known as Firefox Quantum).

Get Ready

Once Clippings is upgraded to the new WebExtension, there is an extra step you will need to take to bring your clippings over. To prepare for the upgrade, you should save a backup of your clippings.

Hint

You can backup your clippings as many times as you want, until Clippings is upgraded to version 6.

You may want to do this to ensure you have the most up-to-date backup.

Backup Your Clippings

  1. Open Clippings Manager.
  2. Click Tools → Export.
  3. Ensure "Clippings File" is selected, then click Export.
  4. Select a location on your system to export to (such as the Desktop or your Documents folder), then proceed with the export.

Make sure you remember where you exported your clippings to. You will need to locate the exported clippings file once Clippings is upgraded.

» Click here to open Clippings Manager and save a backup now.

After Upgrading to Clippings 6

Once Clippings 6 is released, Firefox may automatically upgrade your existing Clippings installation.

After the upgrade is complete, follow the on-screen instructions to bring your clippings over to Clippings 6, and you'll be up and running again.
